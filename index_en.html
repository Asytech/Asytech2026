Frequently asked questions

🔒 Are my data stored on an external server?
No, absolutely not. All data (badges, history) remain on your phone and on the door. No Internet connection is required.
🔋 What is the battery life?
Up to 6 months with standard batteries. Low‑level alerts are sent to your app.
📱 Can I control multiple doors with the same app?
Yes, the app can manage an unlimited number of AsyTech devices.
🐄 Does LoRa technology work without Internet?
Yes, the LoRa network is completely local. The gateway communicates via Bluetooth with your phone when you are nearby.
